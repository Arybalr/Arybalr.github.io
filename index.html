About Us

NORE is at the forefront of cutting-edge technology, delivering Virtual Reality (VR) and Augmented Reality (AR) solutions to help organizations prepare for critical, high-pressure scenarios. Our mission is to revolutionize training by making it safer, more effective, and highly engaging.

What We Do

At NORE, we design custom simulations that replicate real-world situations with remarkable detail. Our services include:

  • Nuclear plant emergency simulations
  • Airport security and in-flight emergency training
  • School intruder preparedness
  • Emergency response and disaster management

How We Do It

NORE combines advanced VR and AR hardware with sophisticated software to create seamless, immersive experiences. Our process includes:

  1. Collaborating with industry experts to understand unique challenges.
  2. Designing realistic scenarios tailored to your organization.
  3. Integrating AI-driven dynamic responses to enhance training realism.
  4. Providing ongoing support and updates for your simulations.

Who We Help

NORE works with a diverse range of industries and organizations, including:

  • Nuclear Power Plants: Preparing for critical, high-risk situations.
  • Airport Security Teams: Simulating real-time threats and responses.
  • Schools: Training staff and students for intruder and emergency scenarios.
  • Emergency Responders: Enhancing decision-making and readiness for disasters.

Real-World Examples

The effectiveness of VR technology in training is proven by its adoption by leading companies across various industries:

Delta Air Lines

Uses VR to train flight attendants in handling in-flight emergencies, including evacuations and passenger safety.

Johnson & Johnson

Trains surgeons with VR simulations to enhance precision and improve surgical outcomes while reducing costs.

Varjo & Boeing

Collaborated to develop VR-based astronaut training for the International Space Station (ISS), simulating zero-gravity environments.
