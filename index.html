About Us

NORE is at the forefront of cutting-edge technology, delivering Virtual Reality (VR) and Augmented Reality (AR) solutions to help organizations prepare for critical, high-pressure scenarios. Our mission is to revolutionize training by making it safer, more effective, and highly engaging.

What We Do

At NORE, we design custom simulations that replicate real-world situations with remarkable detail. Our services include:

  • Nuclear plant emergency simulations
  • Airport security and in-flight emergency training
  • School intruder preparedness
  • Emergency response and disaster management

How We Do It

NORE combines advanced VR and AR hardware with sophisticated software to create seamless, immersive experiences. Our process includes:

  1. Collaborating with industry experts to understand unique challenges.

  3. Integrating AI-driven dynamic responses to enhance training realism.

Benefits of VR Training

VR training offers remarkable advantages, making it safer, more effective, and engaging for employees.

Simulate Realistic Environments

Create training environments that are immersive and practical, ensuring lessons are safer, more effective, and better for employee retention.

Reduce Risk

From surgery to firefighting to pilot training, simulate high-risk scenarios in a controlled, low-risk environment.

Increase Engagement

VR eliminates distractions by immersing users completely in the content, helping them stay super engaged with the learning process.

Learn Faster

Emotional connections created by VR enhance memory retention, making learning not just realistic but also unforgettable.

233% higher scores: Surgeons trained in VR at Johnson & Johnson achieved 233% better outcomes compared to passive learning tools.

63% of VR learners absorb information faster, and 59% complete hard skills training more efficiently than traditional e-learners.
